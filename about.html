About Me

My Story

Hi, I’m Andrew. I’m a student at Career Foundry studying UX Design. I enjoy taking complex problems and turning them into simple, beautiful and practical designs. Before studying UX design, I spent several years working in sales and marketing for small startups where gained valuable negotiation and presenting skills. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I was made redundant and so I decided that it was time to take the leap into UX design. What I love most about UX Design is the need to empathise with people and the creativity in bringing designs to life. Along with my studies, I am currently working as a contract UX Designer at Pharmacy Mentor. And finally, when I’m not designing, I also work as a part-time fitness instructor for Be Military Fit in London.
